Langley carvolth

Carvolth Exchange is a major bus loop and park and ride facility serving the northern portion of the Township of Langley. Opened on December 1, 2012, Carvolth Exchange's primary connections are to Vancouver (via Burnaby), Surrey and Langley City all operated by TransLink. Since April 6, 2015, BC Transit has operated the Fraser Valley Express to Abbotsford and Chilliwack.
