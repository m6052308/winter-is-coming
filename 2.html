Langley centre

Langley Centre is the major public transit exchange serving the City of Langley and the Township of Langley in British Columbia, Canada. Opened on October 31, 1975, it is home to Langley's bus routes and community shuttle routes; some of Langley's bus routes connect to the SkyTrain system at Surrey Central station.
